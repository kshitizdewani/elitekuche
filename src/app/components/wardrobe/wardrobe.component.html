Hinged wardrobes

Wardrobes with shutters attached on hinges are hinged wardrobes. Hinged wardrobes can be customized to any shape and design. These conventional design wardrobes are always very easy to use and low on maintenance. These are available in variety of front finishes and handle design choices.

Sliding wardrobes

Wardrobes with horizontal siding system shutters are sliding wardrobes. These wardrobes are essentially designed for full height and clear width storage when widely opened. These are space savers in the bedroom and can be customized as per your style statement. Sliding wardrobes are available in variety of front finishes and handle design choices.

Walk-in Wardrobes

Wardrobes with open walk-in space are very modern and luxurious choice of wardrobes. Walk-in wardrobes are aesthetically very smart and elegant. These can be customized and designed in any open bedroom space. If you have the walk-in wardrobe, that’s surely the favorite part of your home.
